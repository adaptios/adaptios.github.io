Adaptios Discount Manager Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information is accessed, used, and protected when you install or use Adaptios Discount Manager (the “App”).

By installing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to it.

2. Information Accessed

The App accesses and processes limited store-related data made available through the Shopify platform, including:

  • Product information and pricing
  • Campaign configuration and scheduling data
  • Store identifiers, such as a unique store ID
  • Technical data required for the App to function, including session data and webhooks
  • Basic store information necessary for functionality and support

The App does not intentionally collect or store customer payment information or sensitive personal data. The App operates as a service provider to Shopify merchants and may process data on behalf of the merchant in accordance with the purposes outlined above.

3. No Direct Relationship with Store Visitors

The App does not have a direct relationship with your customers or visitors.

You are responsible for ensuring that your store’s privacy disclosures and permissions comply with applicable laws in your jurisdiction. We do not control or determine how merchants collect or use their customers’ personal information.

4. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Apply pricing adjustments
  • Store campaign snapshots
  • Provide support
  • Maintain system security
  • Improve the reliability of the App

Information is not sold. Personal information is not shared with third parties except where necessary to operate the App, such as Shopify infrastructure providers, or where legally required.

Information is used strictly for the purpose of providing the App’s functionality and is not used for independent marketing, profiling, or resale.

5. Data Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to safeguard data, taking into account industry standards and the nature of the information processed.

Data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 of South Africa (POPIA) and, where applicable, international data protection frameworks.

6. Data Retention

Campaign-related data may be retained for a limited period, currently up to 3 months, to support functionality, reporting, and system reliability.

Data may be deleted earlier upon request, subject to technical limitations. Where required, reasonable steps will be taken to delete stored information.

7. Data Hosting & Cross-Border Transfers

Data may be processed and stored on infrastructure located outside your country, including servers in the United States, for example AWS us-east-1.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of data across borders where necessary for the operation of the service.

Where required, we take reasonable steps to ensure that such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards.

8. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your store-related data by contacting support.

While we take reasonable steps to delete data upon request, certain technical data, including webhook-related records, may persist temporarily due to system limitations.

9. Business Transfers

If the business is sold, merged, or restructured, relevant information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to confidentiality protections.

10. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised version.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact support at adaptios@outlook.com.
